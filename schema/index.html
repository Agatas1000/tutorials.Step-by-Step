A simple "supermarket" NGSI-LD Data Model consisting of 4 entities:

  • Product - something sold in a Store
  • Shelf - a unit of Store Furniture. Products are found on a shelf
  • StockOrder - an entry in a list of items sold in one or more stores
  • Store - a building which sells things.
